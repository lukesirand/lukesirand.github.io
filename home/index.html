About Luke Sirand

My name is Luke Sirand and I am a student at New West Charter Highschool in Los Angeles California from 9th to 12th grade. I am an epee fencer who competes at the national level, fencing at hte Junior Olympics. I have been committed to the sport for the past 6 years and plan to continue throughout college. I am a bass clarinet and saxophone player in the school orchestra and after school jazz band respectively. I have been very committed to music, playing the clarinet since 6th grade and saxophone since 9th grade. For hobbies, I actively take photographs, explore fashion, and learn computer science (I developed this webpage with HTML and CSS). I am also part of a student focus group that helped the orginization "Go For Broke" develope and open a museum exhibit in Downtown Los Angeles.
